    What is RLCS?

    The Rocket League Championship Series is the official Rocket league E-sport tournament which takes place annually between the best teams around the world. The format is a 3v3 tournament where two teams play to win 4 games each match with three qualification splits across different regions.

    How does it work?

    At first there is an open wildcard stage with a 16 team swiss format (non eliminating points league) with the top 8 in 2 different brackets proceeding to the group stage. Teams earn points towards qualifying for the RLCS LAN finals. In the LAN stage there is a group stage with 16 teams split into 2 groups of 8 in a double elimination bracket. The top 4 teams in each group (8 in total) will finally progress to the playoffs in a traditional knockout format.

    How does a team win?

    To win the whole series a team must qualify for the LAN, pass the group stage, win the quarter finals, semi finals and the final. The reward is a cash prize pool ($6 million in RLCS season X - 2021) for the team along with a trophy.
